about me

welcome

Hi! Pleasure to meet you.

I spent a good few years after graduating from University in pretty menial jobs, saving up for a few big trips travelling, volunteering and working abroad.

I got back to the UK in 2017 and became an adult, I now have a ring on my finger, two kids at my feet and a mortgage on my back. For the last 4 years I've been working for a Complex Needs Housing charity and as a Family Support Worker for a Special Needs School. Both have been amazing and invaluable to my personal development but I needed a new challenge. That leads me here!

I stumbled into software development after two close friends made bootcamp led career changes. I dabbled with some self learning and found myself completely drawn to the practical, organised, meticulous nature of creating, building and testing software. I've now started the course and have honestly loved it. I know already that I've made the right choice, I now can't wait to get that first job and start applying what I know and learning new things.

Outside of work I'm Daddy Shark. A Yogi. I love to read. I try to run. And I'm pretty sure I had some other interesting hobbies before having kids. Little darlings.
